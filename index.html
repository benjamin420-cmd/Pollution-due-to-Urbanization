Welcome

Urbanization has led to significant pollution problems worldwide. This website explores the causes, effects, and potential solutions to this growing issue.

Causes of Pollution

Urbanization contributes to pollution through industrial activities, vehicular emissions, and improper waste management.

Effects of Pollution

Pollution from urbanization affects air quality, water sources, and soil, leading to health problems and environmental degradation.

Solutions to Pollution

Effective waste management, promoting green spaces, and using cleaner energy sources are some ways to combat pollution due to urbanization.
